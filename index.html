E-learning is a distance learning process based on multimedia resources, which allows one or more people to learn from their computer. The media used can combine text, 2 or 3 dimensional graphics, sound, picture, animation and even video. These supports make it possible to revolutionize the teaching approach, to use more fun methods where interactivity plays a big role, to diversify the tools used, to adapt more to the learning process of the learner, who becomes the pilot of his training. The latter will be able to train at his own pace, according to his needs and availability, which is particularly important at a time when training takes place throughout life.

About us





Our website is an online learning platform founded in 2020 which offers its members certifying courses and pathways leading to growing professions. Its content is produced internally, by schools, universities, partner companies such as Microsoft or IBM, or historically by volunteers.

E-learning.tn courses last approximately four to twelve weeks, with one to two hours of video lectures a week. These courses provide quizzes, weekly exercises, peer-graded assignments, an optional Honors assignment and sometimes a final project or exam. Courses are also provided on-demand, in which case users can take their time in completing the course with all of the material available at once.
